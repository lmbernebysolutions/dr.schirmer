## Photography Guidelines

### Technical Requirements
- **Resolution:** Minimum 300 DPI for print, 72 DPI for web
- **Format:** JPEG or PNG
- **Color Profile:** sRGB
- **File Size:** Optimized for web (under 500KB per image)

### Style Guidelines
- **Lighting:** Natural light preferred, professional lighting acceptable
- **Composition:** Clean, uncluttered backgrounds
- **Colors:** Consistent with practice branding (yellow/red theme)
- **Mood:** Professional, welcoming, trustworthy

### Consent and Privacy
- **Patient Privacy:** No patients visible in photos
- **Staff Consent:** Written consent from all staff members
- **Data Protection:** Compliance with GDPR requirements
